# Risks and mitigations

## Risk 1: treatment timing ambiguity
Mitigation:
- store exact effective dates,
- use preferred and alternative quarterly treatment definitions.

## Risk 2: donor-pool sensitivity
Mitigation:
- pre-specify an initial donor pool,
- run leave-one-out checks,
- document exclusions.

## Risk 3: mixing incomparable policy regimes
Mitigation:
- do not collapse all rent-control forms into one national binary treatment in phase 1.

## Risk 4: occupied rents vs market rents
Mitigation:
- interpret ACS as incumbent / occupied rent evidence,
- defer asking-rent work to extensions.

## Risk 5: over-claiming from recent Washington treatment
Mitigation:
- treat Washington as descriptive extension only.

## Risk 6: getting trapped in data archaeology
Mitigation:
- follow the phase plan,
- honor the stop rules in `config/project_settings.yml`.
